Clear skies over The Netherlands gave the perfect opportunity to view the
transit of the planet  Mercury across the face of the sun yesterday. 

The following photo was taken at 13:34 CET with a 90 mm refracting telescope
fitted with a solar  filter and hooked up to an i-Phone. 

_2016 May 10_
