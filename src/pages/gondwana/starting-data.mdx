The work described here addresses two questions: 

(1). How did the constituent continents of Gondwana originally fit togetehr precisely?
(2). Can we define precisely the pathways by which the continents dispersed to the reach their present-day positions?

The global data, geophysical and geological, that provided the constraint to building our geodynamic maodel are illustrated graphically in the above presentation.
