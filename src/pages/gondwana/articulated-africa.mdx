## Articulated Africa

![Cretaceous Reshaping](http://www.reeves.nl/upload/animafrica-1.gif)

A lot of changes took place in a short time interval in the Early Cretaceous. The unwrapping of other fragments of Gondwana from around Africa in a clockwise direction accelerated
as India started to break out from its position between Australia, Antarctica and Madagascar.
