## Some useful reference material

This page gives access to items that should help understand the figures and animations on this site and assist those who wish to implement the rotation models 
on their own plate-reconstruction software. Queries?  [Please get in touch](../../contact)

* [Legend to the animations:](../legend-to-the-anims) Explanation of the colours used in the figures and animations
* [Recent rotation poles:](../latest-rotation) Euler rotation parameters for the most recent reconstruction model
* [Finite Euler rotation poles for Gondwana reassembly](http://www.reeves.nl/upload/ResearchUpdateNo14.pdf) - in need of updating
* [Course Notes, Rio de Janiero 2015](http://www.reeves.nl/upload/Course-Notes-Rio-2015.pdf) Notes for a course of 12 lectures entitled *African Geodynamics*.
* [Reeves & Teasdale, 2019:](../pesgb-africa-2019) *The creation of the African margins and the Mesozoic demise of Gondwana*
* [Reeves, 2020:](../nacgeo-march-2020) *African geology, the Bouvet mantle plume and the early opening of the Gondwana margins*
* [Reeves & Souza, 2021:](../nacgea-april-2021) *The lost 'continents' of the South Atlantic Ocean*
* [Reeves, 2022:](https://www.reeves.nl/upload/nac2022-cvr.pdf) *A comprehensive model for the growth of the oceans that separated Gondwana using fracture-zone retracing*
