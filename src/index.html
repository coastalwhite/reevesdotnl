About Colin Reeves
Colin Reeves is an earth scientist specialised in geophysical mapping. In a career spanning more than 50 years he has worked primarily in Africa and the southern hemisphere on national airborne survey programmes. In retirement he endeavours to share these unique insights and realise their scientific value to studies of Gondwana and its dispersal.
